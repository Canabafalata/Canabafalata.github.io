Damian Lillard

Damian Lamonte Ollie Lillard Sr. is an American professional basketball player for the Portland Trail Blazers. Damian Lillard graduated from Weber State University on a basketball scholarship and then was drafted 6th in the 2012 NBA draft. Damian Lillard is one of the top point guards in the NBA league and is known for his deep three point shooting.
